006 — Creative Journey

The Story
So Far.

00
Origin — The Obsession

Born Restless

My creative journey didn't start with a business plan — it started with an obsession. A need to make things that felt wrong in all the right ways. The ordinary was never enough. I was always drawn to the edges of visual culture; the places where the rules dissolve and genuine creative energy ignites. From day one, the question wasn't "what does the client want?" — it was "what has never been seen before?"

01
2016 – 2018

The Craft

Deep, relentless dives into typography, colour theory, and frame-by-frame animation. Building the foundation of a visual language that would become unmistakably mine — disciplined yet disobedient.

02
2021

Going Global

The work began travelling beyond Nairobi. Campaigns, brand films, and animated identities for clients who wanted something they had never seen before — and trusted me to deliver it.

03
2024

The Elevation

Formalising the obsession. Taking on massive agency roles, executing national BTL and ATL campaigns, and proving that art and storytelling that disobey the ordinary can thrive at the highest corporate levels.

04
Now — Always

The Next Chapter

Bigger briefs. Bolder visions. I'm not chasing trends — I'm setting the tone for what creative culture looks like when it refuses to play it safe. The story continues.
