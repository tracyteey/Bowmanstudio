001 — About Me

I've always asked
too many
questions.

Why does this ad work on me? Why does this colour feel like safety? Why does good design make silence feel full? I fell in love with design when I realized it could answer those questions without saying a word.

My process blends design, direction, and narrative thinking not because it sounds clever, but because people remember meaning more than they remember visuals. Design isn't decoration; it's logic wrapped in emotion.
